ROLE

Introduction

This collection showcases 3 sets of posters I previously created to either present a visual identity (poster 1) or communicate a speculative design concept (posters 2&3). These posters highlight my visual design skills of layout, typography, visual identity, and image treatment.

*This collection is a quick showcase of my visual design and doesn’t contain the design process of these posters. To view my design projects with the full process explained, please click the link: Van Bike App Design, Tool Library Showcase, Brandstorm 2020.

Summer 2021, Course: User Experience Design. Identity Design for a Jazz festival. Teammate: Dony L, Pranav S, Cassandra G.
Inspired by Wolfgang Weingart's Swiss Punk Typography. The visual style is to manipulate the scale and rotation of the letters in an unusual way to create an intriguing reading flow. Fall 2021, Course: Speculative Design. Through the speculation of a reactive leather wallet, the work aims to raise awareness of animal welfare in the leather industry.
Fall 2021, Course: Speculative Design. Poster designed with teammate David W.

The poster advertises a mask that auto-adjusted to the average face size of all users. The work gives a satirical critic on the one-size-fits-all solution in the traditional design practices.

The product render comes from XUPERMASK, it was retouched to communicate our concept. Reference: : https://will.i.am/
