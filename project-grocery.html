Conclusion

This web design is a practice of branding for a business, it successfully presents a unique visual identity for an online grocery, including using appropriate colors and logo that represent the feel of a grocery shop. I considered all the user flows that are required in a smooth shopping experience and created possible pages with a beautiful design.

Also, I took part in developing the website. It provides the hands-on experience of developing all the required functions of an online business, as well as collaborating with another teammate using Github.

Reflection

In peer review, my colleagues suggested to add more information in the product detail page, so the website can provide customers adequate product information to trust the grocery product and purchase. Besides, I believe the color and spacing in the home page can still be improved to enhance the visual hierarchy in the next step of refining.
